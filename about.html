My Background

I'm currently based in Milwaukee, WI, USA, and I've lived in the area my entire life.

From my school days up until today, I've always been known by family and friends as being interested in and curious about computers. I've spent countless hours throughout my life on the computer exploring and creating.

Producing an animated music video to Weird Al's "Albuquerque" and building a "Dream" in Furcadia with functionality coded in DragonSpeak, the game's native scripting language, are just a couple of the personal projects I worked on when I was a kid. As an adult, I continue to enjoy completing similarly fun and functional projects.

My interests away from the world of screens include epic journeys across the city on my bicycle, disc golf, tennis, yoga, fiction novels, traveling, and trying any and every kind of beer and wine.
